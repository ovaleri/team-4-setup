Welcome to Food For Thought!

Created by six passionate ASU students, Food For Thought is your ultimate solution to the age-old question: "What should I eat?"

Our Story

As college students, we understood the daily struggle of deciding what to eat. Whether you're pressed for time, working with limited ingredients, or just can't make up your mind, Food For Thought is here to help. Our team of six ASU students came together with a shared vision: to create an innovative solution that makes meal decisions easier and more enjoyable for everyone.

What We Offer

Smart Recipe Finder

Answer a few simple questions about your preferences, dietary restrictions, and available ingredients, and let our intelligent system suggest the perfect meal for you.

Comprehensive Search

Browse through our extensive database of recipes, filter by cuisine type, preparation time, or dietary requirements to find exactly what you're craving.

User Profiles

Create your personal profile to save favorite recipes, track your meal history, and receive personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

Key Features

  • Personalized Recommendations: Our algorithm learns your preferences to suggest meals you'll love.
  • Quick Decision Making: Answer a few questions and get instant meal suggestions.
  • Recipe Search: Easily search through our database using filters and keywords.
  • User Accounts: Save your favorites and track your meal history.
  • Mobile Friendly: Access Food For Thought anywhere, anytime.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Create your account to get started.
  2. Answer Questions: Tell us about your preferences and restrictions.
  3. Get Recommendations: Receive personalized meal suggestions.
  4. Save Favorites: Keep track of meals you love.
